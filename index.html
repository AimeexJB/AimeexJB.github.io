From brand new opportunities to existing projects that need new life, whatever your challenge we have a workshop to suit and a team eager to help.

Workshop 1. Accelerator

Do you need to move fast? Is your market being disrupted or your competitor gaining traction with new offerings? Is an operation within your business screaming out for improvement?

This full day workshop is designed to look at what’s next for your company and what products and services need to happen now to succeed. This workshop will draw out the user/customer needs, expectations, operating landscape and technical readiness. It will also cover key enablers to get started and how best to deliver fast.

Workshop 2. Pioneer

Do you have an opportunity to lead in your market with a brand new idea? Do you need help going through the opportunity and prioritising activity around market, brand and capability fit?

This full day workshop will unpack a pioneering idea or opportunity and consider the customer, commercial and experience benefit of launching it. It was also consider just what might be needed by way of resources to grab that opportunity and run with it all the way to market release.

Workshop 3. Innovate

Is there an existing product or service in your portfolio that needs modernisation or is ripe for innovation? Could we apply design thinking to identify areas of innovation and improvement?

This full day workshop will consider an existing product or service in your portfolio, draw out the potential areas for innovation and growth and we will blueprint together the priorities and programme or work needed to drive that innovation.
